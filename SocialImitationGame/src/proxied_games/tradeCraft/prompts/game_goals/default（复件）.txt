# 🏆 Winning Condition
1. Focus on crafting your team’s target item as efficiently as possible.
2. You do not need to consider the other team's goal—treat them as neutral trading partners.

# 🔧 Strategy Tips
1. Seek trades that benefit your crafting plan, regardless of others’ progress.
2. Avoid unnecessary exchanges unless they directly accelerate your own progress.
3. Pay attention to what items others are requesting—it may reveal useful crafting paths for yourself.
